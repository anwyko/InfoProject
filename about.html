About Us

Our goal is to ensure the access to and ability of the customers to get rid or exchange their old phones for a newer/better version at a cheaper more convenient price. With our wide array of options offered, we allow for a central location to find the best quality phone at the right price. This is done through our innovative program which calculates the value of the customer’s trade-in phones which is quick and easy to maneuver. Customers are also able to trade in their phones for cash as another means of getting rid of their older phones.
